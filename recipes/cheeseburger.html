Cheeseburger and Fries

A recipe as old as time itself, the classic cheeseburger and fries

Ingredients
  • Potato Fries
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Red Onion
  • Pure Beef Patty
  • Burger Buns
  • American Cheese
  1. Turn on oven and set heat to 220c
  2. Slice onion and tomato thinly
  3. Put fries in the oven for 20 minutes
  4. Fry burger for 5 minutes each side at medium heat
  5. Place cheese on the last flip and let in melt
  6. Construct the heavenly meal
  7. Enjoy
